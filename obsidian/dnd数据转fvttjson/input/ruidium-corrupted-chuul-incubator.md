## Traits

- **Amphibious**. The incubator can breathe air and water.
- **Sense Magic**. The incubator can sense magic within 120 feet as if it had the *detect magic* spell cast on it, except this trait isn't magical.
- **Brittle Ruidium Shell**. If the incubator takes 15 or more bludgeoning damage from a single source, its AC becomes 13 until it finishes a short or long rest.
- **Bloodied Brood-Rupture**. The first time the incubator is reduced to 52 HP or fewer, it immediately recharges Ruidium Brood Patch. The next time it uses Ruidium Brood Patch, creatures that fail their saves in the area must also make a Ruidium Corruption save.
- **Ruidium Corruption**. When a creature is required to make a Ruidium Corruption save due to a trait or action of this incubator, the creature must succeed on a DC 14 Charisma saving throw or suffer Ruidium Corruption. If the creature is already affected by Ruidium Corruption, it instead gains 1 level of exhaustion.

## Actions

- **Multiattack**. The incubator makes two attacks: one Seizing Claw and one Crushing Claw. If it's grappling a creature, it can replace Crushing Claw with Tentacles and use Ruidium Brood Patch if available.
- **Seizing Claw**. *Melee Weapon Attack*: +7 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. *Hit*: 11 (2d6 + 4) bludgeoning damage. If the target is Large or smaller, it's grappled (escape DC 15) and pulled up to 5 feet toward the incubator. The incubator can have only one creature grappled this way at a time.
- **Crushing Claw**. *Melee Weapon Attack*: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit*: 13 (2d8 + 4) bludgeoning damage. The target must succeed on a DC 14 Constitution saving throw or have disadvantage on Strength-based attack rolls until the end of its next turn.
- **Tentacles**. A creature grappled by the incubator must succeed on a DC 14 Constitution saving throw or be poisoned until the end of its next turn. If the target is already affected by Ruidium Corruption, it instead becomes paralyzed until the end of its next turn.
- **Ruidium Brood Patch** (Recharge 4-6). The incubator creates a 5-foot-radius brood patch at a point within 30 feet, lasting for one minute. The area becomes difficult terrain. A creature that enters the area for the first time on a turn, or starts its turn there, must succeed on a DC 14 Constitution saving throw or take 6 (1d12) poison damage plus 6 (1d12) psychic damage and have its speed reduced to 0 until the end of the turn. On a successful save, it takes half damage with no other effects.
